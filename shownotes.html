With the holiday season in full effect, we wanted to touch on the subject of boundaries. Boundaries are something that are important not only with ourselves, but also with family, friends, partners, work, projects, co-workers, etc...

Loving yourself is so important, because when we respect and keep ourselves in high regard we are able to put up necessarily boundaries for healthy and fulfilling relationships. Boundaries are a way of respecting ourselves and the people around us.

Boundaries with ourselves can look: being kind to ourselves, giving ourselves time to ground and connect, asking for what we need, voicing opinions, telling loved ones what doesn't feel right and asking/demanding respect.

We talk about what boundaries have been like for each of us, we give examples of how we manage certain situations.
